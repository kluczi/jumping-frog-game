Welcome to Magenta Froggy!
To start: make && ./main 
Press Q to quit.
Press Z to jump on the friendly car.
RED CARS - enemy cars that kill you
YELLOW CARS - neutral cars that will stop when they are close to you, you can jump over them vertically
BLUE CARS - friendly cars that won't kill you but also will take you on the ride after pressing SPACE
0 - holes, if u jump into them u die
